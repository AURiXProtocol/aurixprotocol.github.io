Why this is not surveillance

The same mechanisms that expose dark patterns could be used to shame, score, or punish. The property that prevents that is the compassion constraint: AURiX surfaces without shaming; the affordance is return, not punishment. Every feature is tested against it.

Silence as design. Shape, not score. The artifact is the mechanism, not the verdict. The invariants protect honesty. The compassion constraint protects agency. Both must hold.

Eight invariants

Every signal AURiX surfaces satisfies all eight. If it fails any, it is not AURiX.

  1. Mechanism only — no interpretation
  2. No interpretation imposed
  3. Separation of layers
  4. Observable or declared
  5. Explicit origin
  6. No silent transformation
  7. Reproducibility boundary
  8. No undeclared meaning

If a signal requires judgment, it is not an AURiX signal.

Kindness is a ratio

L = E − D
K = −D / E
EEfficiency. Declared functional paths available. DDistortion. Undeclared mechanisms altering, intercepting, or redirecting. LLogic. What remains after distortion. KKindness. The ratio, not the sentiment.

Logic is efficiency minus distortion. Kindness is a structural property, not a sentiment. When distortion is visible, it becomes reducible.
